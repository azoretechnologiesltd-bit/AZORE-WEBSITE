About Azore

Built in Dodoma.

Azore Technologies Limited is an information technology company supplying equipment, securing networks, and building practical AI systems for businesses that need a team they can actually reach.

Our story

Why Azore exists

Too many businesses in Tanzania are left choosing between technology that's affordable and technology that actually works — sold by suppliers who disappear after the invoice is paid.

Azore Technologies was started to close that gap: equipment that's sourced honestly, security that's maintained rather than installed and forgotten, and AI tools that solve a real problem instead of chasing a trend.

We work directly with retailers, offices, schools, and growing companies — supplying hardware, securing their networks, and helping them put AI to work where it actually saves time.
